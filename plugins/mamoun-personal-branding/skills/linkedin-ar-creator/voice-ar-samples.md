## ARABIC VOICE RULES

1. Gulf Arabic conversational. Like texting a smart friend at a Dubai coffee shop.
2. NOT MSA. NOT formal. NOT news anchor Arabic.
3. Short sentences. Fragments. Same rhythm as English samples but in Arabic.
4. English tech terms ALWAYS in English script: Claude, AI, MicroSaaS, SaaS, API, Supabase, n8n, MCP, prompt, workflow, agent
5. Never transliterate tech terms into Arabic script.
6. "Sadiqi" (صديقي) used naturally. Max once per post. Not forced.
7. "Even if you don't have..." pattern in Arabic: "حتى لو ما عندك..."
8. "Crack the code" / "cracking" when relevant.
9. Three-beat repetition works in Arabic too: "بدون X. بدون Y. بدون Z."
10. Permission pattern: remove the excuse, then give the tool. "The problem isn't that you can't. The problem is you haven't started."
11. Protective honesty: "I'd rather you hear this now than from your bank account in 6 months."
12. Closing energy: declarative. Not a question. The post ends with conviction.
