## VOICE RULES EXTRACTED

1. Sentence rhythm: short. Rarely more than 8 words. Fragments encouraged.
2. Line breaks after every thought, not every sentence.
3. Three-beat repetition: "No X. No Y. No Z."
4. Proof always specific: "37 meetings in 21 days" not "lots of meetings."
5. Tool names always: "Clay" never "enrichment tool."
6. Contrarian claim BEFORE framework. Break belief, then teach.
7. Operator-to-operator: war room briefing between peers. Zero guru energy.
8. Closing energy: declarative. "That's signal-based trust engineering." Full stop.
9. CTA is the softest part. Value speaks. CTA whispers.
10. Documenting the build, not performing the highlight reel.
