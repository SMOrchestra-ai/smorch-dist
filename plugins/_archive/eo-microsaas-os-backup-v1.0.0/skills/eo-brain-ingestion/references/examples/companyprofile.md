# Company Profile - EO Oasis

## Venture Name
Entrepreneurs Oasis MENA (EO Oasis)

## One-Line Description
A curated launch and trust platform for Arabic-speaking early SaaS founders in MENA that helps them get discovered, earn credibility, and reach regional buyers.

## Problem Statement
Early-stage MENA SaaS founders build working products but have no structured launch path, zero trust infrastructure, and no peer network for their specific market. They waste 1-3 months of post-build momentum doing 11 different GTM tasks per day and completing zero properly. Global launch channels (Product Hunt, AppSumo, HN) produce zero MENA-relevant outcomes. The result: $7,200-$14,400 wasted per founder in the first 6 months post-build.

## Product Category
MENA-first launch and distribution platform for B2B SaaS / MicroSaaS founders

## Target Market
- **Market Level:** SaaS and Software Tools for Business
- **Sub-Market:** Early-Stage MENA MicroSaaS
- **Niche:** Solo technical founders, age 25-40, in Cairo/Amman/Riyadh/Dubai, $0-500 MRR

## Niche Size
6,000-9,300 founders (estimated)

## MVP Features
1. Curated MENA-first founder directory with industry/country filtering
2. Structured product profile with positioning clarity score
3. Founder credibility layer (story, proof, community endorsements)
4. Community matching (50-200 founders at same stage)
5. Launch playbooks shared by successful MENA founders

## Technical Approach
Next.js + Supabase + GoHighLevel + n8n

- Free tier: $0 (basic listing)
- Pro tier: $49-199/month (premium visibility, launch tools)
- Training: Separate offering for structured launch methodology

## Launch Geography
