# Wedge Sentence Map

ICP-specific templates for creating one-sentence wedges from signals.

## Core Wedge Formula

```
[Observed Signal] is costing you [Specific Outcome]. [One-Line Solution].
```

**Rules**:
1. Must be ONE sentence (can use periods for rhythm, but core message = one sentence)
2. Include specific data/metric when possible (8x, 21x, $79 vs $582, etc.)
3. Outcome must be tangible (not vague "better results")
4. Solution must be clear (not "we can help")

## ICP-Specific Wedge Templates

### MENA SaaS Founders

**Context**: Trust-first culture, relationship-driven, high-context communication, WhatsApp-native

**Template Patterns**:

#### Pattern 1: Data Fragmentation
```
Signal: "[Tools/data scattered across X systems]"
Wedge: "Your [asset] across [X tools] is creating [specific pain]. [Outcome solution] = [specific result]."

Examples:
- "Your sales data across 7 tools is creating decision paralysis. Unified dashboard = predictable revenue."
- "Pipeline visibility disappeared across HubSpot, Pipedrive, and 4 spreadsheets. One view brings it back."
```

#### Pattern 2: Revenue Leakage
```
Signal: "[Losing/missing revenue opportunity]"
Wedge: "[Current approach] generates [low metric]. [Better approach] generates [high metric]. That's [X]x ROI."

Examples:
- "Instagram followers generate $79 per 1000. Owned contacts generate $582. That's 8x ROI you're leaving on the table."
- "Social engagement without capture = $79. Capture system with owned list = $582. 8x difference."
```

#### Pattern 3: Geographic Distribution
```
Signal: "[Sales team/customers across multiple MENA cities]"
Wedge: "Your [team/asset] is scattered across [cities]. Your [system] isn't. [Regional solution] is the missing piece."

Examples:
- "Your sales team is scattered across Dubai, Riyadh, and Cairo. Your CRM isn't. Regional dashboards are the missing piece."
- "Customers in 5 MENA markets. Support team in one. Regional response system closes the gap."
```

#### Pattern 4: Proof Gap (Silence Type)
```
Signal: "[Making claims without evidence]"
Wedge: "Your [claim] is [adjective]. Your [proof] is [lacking]. [ICP peers] need to see it working."

Examples:
- "Your 10x growth claim is bold. Your case studies are silent. MENA founders need to see other MENA founders."
- "Unified data sounds great. Proof is missing. Show me 3 SaaS companies who did it."
```

### US Real Estate Brokers

**Context**: Speed-driven, direct communication, mobile-first, impatient with complexity

**Template Patterns**:

#### Pattern 1: Golden Window (Response Speed)
```
Signal: "[Slow response to leads]"
Wedge: "Responding after [X time] costs you [Y]x in conversion. The first [golden window] is everything."

Examples:
- "Responding after 30 minutes costs you 21x in conversion. The first 5 minutes is everything."
- "Your lead fills a form at 2pm. You reply at 5pm. They booked with someone else at 2:07pm."
```

#### Pattern 2: Lead Leakage
```
Signal: "[Leads falling through cracks]"
Wedge: "[X]% of your leads say '[objection].' [Y]% will [convert] after [touchpoints]. You're missing the [opportunity]."

Examples:
- "98% of your leads say 'I'll think about it.' 80% will book after 5-12 touchpoints. You're missing the backend 50x improvement."

```

#### Pattern 3: Listing Capture
```
Signal: "[Open houses/showings without capture mechanism]"
Wedge: "[X people] walked through your open house. [Y captured]. [Z revenue] lost."

Examples:
- "150 people walked through your open house. 12 captured. $2.1M in potential commission lost."
- "Every showing without capture = invisible buyer. Capture system = warm pipeline."
```

#### Pattern 4: Mobile Friction
```
Signal: "[Desktop-only systems, complex processes]"
Wedge: "[X]% of your buyers are mobile-first. Your [process] is desktop-locked. That's friction silence."

Examples:
- "67% of your buyers are mobile-first. Your showing request form has 14 fields. That's why they're ghosting you."
- "Mobile buyers don't fill long forms. They text. WhatsApp capture = 3x conversion."
```

## Signal Subtype → Wedge Mapping

**Observable behaviors**:
- "Data lives in X different tools"
- "Can't see full pipeline"
- "Spreadsheet chaos"
- "Everything is scattered"

**Wedge focus**: Unification, consolidation, single source of truth

**Example wedges**:
- "Your sales data across 7 tools is creating decision paralysis. Unified dashboard = predictable revenue."
- "Pipeline visibility disappeared across multiple systems. One dashboard brings it back."
- "Scattered data = invisible revenue. Consolidated view = forecasting confidence."

---

**Observable behaviors**:
- "Can't see what's working"
- "No visibility into performance"
- "Blind spots in funnel"
- "Don't know where leads come from"

**Wedge focus**: Transparency, dashboards, real-time visibility

**Example wedges**:

## Wedge A/B Variant Patterns

Once you have a core wedge, create A/B variants by changing psychological angle:

### Variant Pattern 1: Data vs Story

**Variant A (Data-driven)**:
- "Instagram followers generate $79 per 1000. Owned contacts generate $582. That's 8x ROI."

**Variant B (Story-driven)**:
- "You built 10k Instagram followers. Then your account got suspended. All gone. Owned list survives platform risk."

---

### Variant Pattern 2: Loss Aversion vs Gain Seeking

**Variant A (Loss aversion)**:
- "Responding after 30 minutes costs you 21x in conversion. Every delayed reply = lost deal."

**Variant B (Gain seeking)**:
- "Responding within 5 minutes produces 21x higher conversion. The first 5 minutes is your biggest opportunity."

---

### Variant Pattern 3: Peer Proof vs Self-Discovery

**Variant A (Peer proof)**:
- "5 MENA SaaS founders unified their stack. They closed 40% more deals. Your scattered data is the bottleneck."

**Variant B (Self-discovery)**:
- "How many tools hold your sales data? Count them. Now imagine seeing it all in one place. That's the unlock."

"Your sales data across 7 tools is creating decision paralysis. Unified dashboard = predictable revenue. 147 B2B SaaS companies saw 40% faster deal cycles after consolidation."

**Characteristics**:
- "You" language (individual)
- Direct problem statement

- Outcome-focused ("predictable revenue")

"Multiple studies demonstrate that sales data fragmentation across disparate systems correlates with a 34% reduction in forecast accuracy. Consolidation to a unified analytical dashboard restores visibility and enables evidence-based decision-making."

**Characteristics**:

## Wedge Testing Framework

### Test 1: One-Sentence Rule
Can you state the wedge in one sentence without hedging?
- ✅ PASS: "Your sales data across 7 tools is creating decision paralysis."
- ❌ FAIL: "Your sales data might be scattered, which could potentially create some challenges..."

### Test 2: Specificity Test
Does the wedge include specific data, metric, or observable signal?
- ✅ PASS: "Instagram followers generate $79 per 1000. Owned contacts generate $582."
- ❌ FAIL: "Social media doesn't work as well as email."

### Test 3: Outcome Clarity Test
Is the outcome tangible and quantifiable?
- ✅ PASS: "Unified dashboard = predictable revenue"
- ❌ FAIL: "You'll see better results"

### Test 4: Solution Clarity Test
Is the one-line solution clear and actionable?
- ✅ PASS: "Responding within 5 minutes produces 21x higher conversion."
- ❌ FAIL: "We can help you improve response times."

### Test 5: ICP Resonance Test
Does this wedge use ICP-specific language and context?
- ✅ PASS (MENA SaaS): "Founders across Dubai, Riyadh, Abu Dhabi..."
- ❌ FAIL (Generic): "Business leaders everywhere..."

## Summary: Wedge Creation Workflow

1. **Identify signal** (from signal-detector sub-skill)
2. **Classify signal type** (Trust vs Intent, Subtype)
3. **Apply Intent > Trust rule** (if both present, use Intent)
4. **Select ICP template** (MENA SaaS, US Real Estate, etc.)
5. **Insert signal data** (specific numbers, observed behavior)
6. **State outcome** (8x ROI, 21x conversion, $X recovered)
7. **One-line solution** (Unified dashboard, Golden Window automation, etc.)
8. **Test against 5 criteria** (one-sentence, specificity, outcome clarity, solution clarity, ICP resonance)
9. **Create A/B variants** (data vs story, loss vs gain, peer vs self)
10. **Geographic adaptation** (if multi-market campaign)

**Result**: Signal-specific, ICP-resonant, one-sentence wedge ready for asset-factory.
