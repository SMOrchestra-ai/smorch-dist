## Competitive Alternatives

Lifetime deal marketplace. Attracts deal-seekers, not MENA enterprise buyers. Devalues product pricing. No Arabic support.

LinkedIn DMs, cold emails, WhatsApp messages. Unstructured, non-repeatable. 11 tasks per day, zero completed properly. Default behavior for 90%+ of MENA founders.

Equity-dilutive. 3-6 month programs. Good for fundraising, weak on distribution mechanics. Not accessible to bootstrapped solo founders.
