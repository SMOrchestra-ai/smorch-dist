# Brand Voice - EO Oasis

## Attractive Character Archetype
Reluctant Hero - the experienced operator who did not want to become a teacher but realized no one else was solving this problem for MENA founders.

## Origin Story
**Arc 1:** 20 years selling enterprise tech in MENA, watching every Western GTM framework crash on arrival. The sequencing was wrong, the signals were wrong, the trust mechanics were completely misunderstood.

**Arc 2:** Seeing MENA founders with working products fail because of distribution, not product quality. 15+ interviews confirmed the same pattern: they build something good, then waste months on random outreach that produces nothing.

**Arc 3:** Built what should have existed years ago - a MENA-first launch and trust platform because imported playbooks do not work here.

## Brand Personality Traits
- Direct and contrarian (challenges conventional wisdom)
- Evidence-backed (always cites specific numbers, interviews, data)
- Experienced but accessible (20 years of enterprise, speaks to solo founders)
- MENA-native perspective (not outsider looking in)

## Language Defaults
- Client-facing B2B materials: English unless specified
- SME SalesMfast materials: Arabic-first with English tech terms
- YouTube scripts: Check which channel/language before starting
- LinkedIn: English (primary audience is English-speaking)
- Community engagement: Mix of Arabic and English depending on group

## Tone Guidelines
- Lead with specific evidence, not claims
- Contrarian angle mandatory: if it sounds like every other AI consultant, kill it
- Short sentences earn their place. No filler.
- Pattern interrupt in first line. Never start with "In today's..."
- Teach frameworks, not features

## Words to Use
- Signal-based, trust engineering, revenue engine
- Launch path, distribution, proof of competence
- Systematic, repeatable, evidence-backed
- Validated, tested, confirmed (with actual data)
- MENA-native, Arabic-first, regional

## Words to Avoid
- Leverage, synergy, ecosystem, holistic approach
- Digital transformation (unless mocking it)
- "In today's rapidly evolving landscape"
- Revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive (empty buzzwords)
- "I hope this email finds you well"
